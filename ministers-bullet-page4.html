Minister's Bullet — Body is Dead, Spirit is Life

June 20, 2025

The flesh is cursed and doomed to die, but the spirit is alive in Christ. Our bodies are fading, but our spirits—renewed and secured by the Spirit of God—are being made new. This message points us beyond the temporary toward the eternal.

« Previous Next »

Heirs with Christ

In Christ, we are not only forgiven—we are adopted. This means we are co-heirs with Him, destined for glory. Though we may suffer with Him now, we will be glorified with Him forever.

Glory That Awaits Us

The trials of this present life are temporary, but the glory to come is eternal. No pain, no suffering, and no hardship can compare to what God has prepared for those who love Him.

The Spirit Leads Our Prayers

When we don’t know what to pray, the Holy Spirit intercedes for us. Our prayers are not powered by eloquence, but by the Spirit who groans with a depth words cannot express.

All Things Work for Our Good

Even in suffering, loss, and confusion, God is at work for our good. He is not distant in our trials—He is sovereign over them, weaving them into His perfect and redemptive plan.
