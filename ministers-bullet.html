The Pride Trap

Pride is subtle—and deadly. It blinds us to correction, hardens us to truth, and distances us from God. In this brief message, we expose how pride creeps into the Christian life and how the gospel calls us to humility instead.

Reaping What We Sow

Every action bears fruit. Whether good or evil, what we plant in our hearts and lives eventually grows and returns to us. This message is a sobering reminder of God's unchanging principle: we reap what we sow.

Don’t Worry

Worry accomplishes nothing—but faith moves mountains. Jesus calls us not to be anxious, but to trust in our Father who knows our needs. In this message, find peace for your heart and a renewed call to trust God completely.

Rightly Handling the Word

Scripture is not ours to shape—it is God's Word to submit to. In a time when truth is often twisted to fit preference, this message is a call to handle the Bible with reverence, accuracy, and integrity.

Negative Self-Image

You are not who the world says you are. In Christ, you are a new creation—loved, chosen, and redeemed. This message confronts the lies of shame and insecurity with the truth of your identity in Jesus.
