A Walk Through Ephesians

Verse-by-verse reflections on Paul’s letter to the church in Ephesus.

Overview

A look at the letter to the Ephesians as a whole—its author, context, purpose, and key themes.

Chapter 1 – Predestination

A bold beginning: Paul explores the eternal plan of salvation and God’s sovereign choice before the foundation of the world.

Chapter 2 – Saved by Grace

We were dead in sin—but God, rich in mercy, made us alive in Christ. Salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone.

Chapter 3 – Mystery Revealed

Paul reveals the great mystery of the gospel: that Gentiles are fellow heirs in Christ. Grace unites and empowers the church.

Chapter 4 – Unity and Maturity

Paul urges believers to walk in a manner worthy of their calling, striving for unity in the Spirit and maturity in Christ.
