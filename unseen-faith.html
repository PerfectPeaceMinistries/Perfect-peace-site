Unseen Faith

Faith is not the absence of sight — it is confidence in what God has spoken. True faith holds fast when the answers haven’t come, when the healing hasn’t arrived, and when the future is unclear.

We walk by faith, not by sight (2 Corinthians 5:7). Our eyes may see trouble, but our hearts rest in God’s promises. He is faithful, even when we don’t feel it. Even when we can’t trace His hand, we trust His heart.

Let this be your peace today: God sees what you can’t. Your faith is not in vain. Trust Him in the silence. Trust Him in the unseen.

"Now faith is the assurance of things hoped for, the conviction of things not seen." — Hebrews 11:1
