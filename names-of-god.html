Names of God

Exploring the Names and Nature of our Lord

YHWH (Yahweh) — I AM WHO I AM

God's personal name revealed to Moses. It speaks of His eternal self-existence and unchanging nature.

Adonai — Lord

A title denoting God's sovereign rule and majestic authority over all creation.

Jehovah — The LORD (Mispronunciation of YHWH)

A later vocalization of God's covenant name YHWH. Originated when scholars mistakenly combined the consonants of YHWH with the vowels of Adonai, resulting in "Jehovah." Though not the original pronunciation, it became common in early Protestant Bible translations.

El Elyon — God Most High

The supreme title for God, declaring Him as sovereign over all creation, exalted above every power and authority.
