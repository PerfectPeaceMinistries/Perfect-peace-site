About This Series

A theological series designed to clarify and celebrate the core doctrines of the Christian faith, written to deepen worship and strengthen assurance in Christ.

The Ordo Salutis: Understanding the Order of Salvation

October 2025 • Doctrine Defined

An introduction to the gracious, logical sequence by which God saves—from election to glorification—so that His mercy might be praised and His grace adored.

Election: God’s Sovereign Choice

October 2025 • Doctrine Defined

A study of God’s sovereign act of choosing His people according to grace, as illustrated through Jacob and Esau in Romans 9, revealing mercy that magnifies His glory.

Regeneration: The New Birth

October 2025 • Doctrine Defined

The sovereign work of the Holy Spirit that makes the sinner alive to God—transforming the heart and bringing forth repentance and faith as the fruit of new life.

Effectual Calling: The Voice That Summons

October 2025 • Doctrine Defined

A reflection on the irresistible grace of God’s inward call—the voice that awakens the heart, opens deaf ears, and draws His chosen ones to faith in Christ.
