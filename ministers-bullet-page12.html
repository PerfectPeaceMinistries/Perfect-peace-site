Minister's Bullet — The Spirit Is a Guarantee

June 20, 2025

God has given us His Spirit as a guarantee—a down payment of the glory that is to come. Though we walk by faith, we do not walk without assurance. The presence of the Spirit is the proof of our adoption, our redemption, and the eternal inheritance awaiting us in Christ.

Are We Bound by the Mosaic Law?

As Christians, we are not under the Mosaic covenant but under grace. Christ fulfilled the Law, and in Him, we are called to walk by the Spirit—not by the letter of the old covenant.
