Minister's Bullet

Only God Is Good

We are not good people who need a little help—we are sinners in need of a Savior. Jesus said, “No one is good except God alone.” This message calls us to lay aside self-righteousness and recognize our desperate need for grace.

True Faith Translates into Obedience

Faith without obedience is not saving faith. True belief in Christ leads to submission, repentance, and a changed life. This message calls us to examine ourselves—not just by what we say we believe, but by how we live.

Salvation Is Through Faith

We are saved by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. No amount of effort, morality, or religious activity can earn salvation. This message lifts high the sufficiency of Christ and the simplicity of trusting in Him.

Rejoice in Our Sufferings

Suffering is not meaningless in the life of a believer. Through trials, God produces endurance, character, and hope. This message reminds us that even in pain, we are being shaped into the image of Christ—and that is cause for rejoicing.

His Power Made Perfect in Weakness

God does not need our strength—He displays His power through our weakness. When we are at our lowest, His grace is sufficient. This message encourages us to stop hiding our frailty and instead boast in it, for there His glory is revealed.

Original Sin

We are not born innocent—we are born in sin. Original sin means every part of our being is affected by the fall. This message explains the weight of our depravity and the glorious grace of the God who saves sinners.

Dead to Sin

If we are in Christ, sin no longer reigns over us. We have died with Him and been raised to walk in newness of life. This message proclaims our freedom—not to sin, but from sin—by the power of the risen Christ.

Minister's Bullet — No Condemnation

June 19, 2025

For those who are in Christ Jesus, there is no condemnation—none. The law could not save us, but Christ did. He bore our guilt and gave us His righteousness. This is the unshakable hope of the gospel.

Minister's Bullet — Do Not Deprive Your Spouse

June 19, 2025

In marriage, intimacy is not a bargaining chip — it’s a gift of love, trust, and unity. Scripture calls husbands and wives to give of themselves freely, not withholding what belongs to the other in covenant faithfulness.
